Chris and Genesis Mattix

Christopher and Genesis Mattix, formerly missionaries to Bolivia for over a decade, are now based out of South Carolina, traveling often to speak at churches, camps, and conferences both nationally and internationally. Christopher has a degree in Bible Exposition and Theology from Emmaus Bible College and is also a bestselling author in Spanish. Genesis has a degree in Business Administration from UAGRM in Bolivia and currently works part-time at Starbucks, which allows her to travel often with her husband as his manager of sales and as a speaker as the occasion arises. They are currently promoting and preparing to co-direct a new docu-series called "Unbreakable Church".
